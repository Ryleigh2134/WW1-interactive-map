About World War 1

World War I, also known as the Great War was a global conflict between two coalitions: the Entente and the Central Powers. The war started in 28 July 1914 due to Serbia denying Austrian demands after a Serb killed the Austrian hier. Fighting lasted 11 November 1918 after Germany and all other Central Power nations surrendered.

The aftermath

By the time the Paris Peace Conference ended which was the formal end of the war the German, Austro-Hungarian, Ottoman, and Russian empires had already been shattered. With this peace many nations found themselves upset or unsatisfied leading to the rise of extremist ideologies and the eventual Second World War.
